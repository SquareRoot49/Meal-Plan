Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the calorie calculations?

Our calorie calculations are based on comprehensive nutritional databases and are accurate to within 5-10% of actual values. However, individual results may vary based on preparation methods and portion sizes.

Can I customize my meal plans?

Yes! You can set dietary preferences, adjust calorie goals, and even swap out individual foods to better suit your taste and schedule.

Do you support special diets?

We support vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and low-carb diets. If you have other dietary restrictions, please contact us and we'll work to accommodate your needs.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously. Your personal information and meal plans are protected using industry-standard encryption and security practices.
